# Investing Research

This folder contains investment research reports produced by Peter, the senior investment analyst agent.

## Structure

Reports are organized by asset class and date:
- `equities/` — Stock and equity research
- `crypto/` — Cryptocurrency analysis
- `macro/` — Macro themes and sector analysis
- `options/` — Options flow and strategy analysis

All reports are for informational purposes only and do not constitute personalized investment advice. Past analysis does not guarantee future results. Always do your own due diligence.
